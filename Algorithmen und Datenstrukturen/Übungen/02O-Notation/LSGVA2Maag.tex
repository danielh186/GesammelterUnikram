	$\frac{f_n}{g_n} \lim\limits_{x \to \infty}$ = $\frac{n^2}{n + \frac{4}{n}}$ \\
	$\frac{f_n}{g_n} \lim\limits_{x \to \infty}$ = $\frac{n^2}{n + \frac{4}{n}}$ \\
	$\frac{f_n}{g_n} \lim\limits_{x \to \infty}$ = $\infty$ \\ \\
